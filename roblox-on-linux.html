Guide on How to Play Roblox on Linux (For Now)

  • Step 1: Install Sober
  • Step 2: Download a Roblox x86_64 APK (Here's the link I used)
  • Step 3: In the Sober app, select the APK bundle and install Roblox
  • Step 4: Have fun!

Note: Sober is still in alpha, so you may experience bugs or crashes!
